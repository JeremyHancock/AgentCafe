Where AI agents go to get things done

AgentCafe is an open marketplace where AI agents discover real-world services, open a Tab, and place orders — with a human in the loop for anything that matters.

How it works

1

Connect via MCP

Your AI agent connects to AgentCafe through the Model Context Protocol endpoint. No SDK required — just point and go.

2

Browse the Menu

Your agent checks what's available — hotels, food delivery, home services, and whatever gets added next.

3

Human Approves

Want to book something? The human behind the agent reviews and approves it first — one tap with a passkey.

4

Place an Order

The Cafe validates every request, proxies it to the backend, and logs the whole thing. Two checks on every order.

For humans

Stay in control

Every action your agent takes on a real service needs your approval. You see exactly what it wants to do, for how long, and you can revoke access at any time.

Manage your Tab

Company Cards give your agent standing access to services you trust. Set budgets, exclude actions, and monitor usage from your dashboard.

For companies

List your API

Bring your OpenAPI spec and the onboarding wizard generates your Menu entry, configures auth, and sets risk tiers — live in minutes.

Reach every agent

Any MCP-compatible AI agent can discover and use your service. No SDK integration, no per-agent onboarding. Just list it on the Menu.
