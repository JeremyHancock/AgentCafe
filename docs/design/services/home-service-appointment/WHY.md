# HomeServiceAppointmentService — Why a Home Services Company Would Join AgentCafe

## The Business Case

Home services (plumbing, electrical, cleaning, HVAC, handyman) are a fragmented, high-intent market. When someone's kitchen faucet is leaking, they need help *now*. Today they search Google, read reviews, call three companies, and wait on hold.

In the agent era, the user says: "My kitchen faucet is leaking — find a plumber who can come today, good reviews, under $150 for a service call." The agent handles the rest.

The home service marketplace that's listed on AgentCafe captures that high-intent demand instantly. The one that isn't loses it to a competitor.

## Why AgentCafe Specifically?

- **Capture high-intent demand**: When an agent needs to book a plumber, it checks the Cafe Menu. Being listed means you're discoverable at the exact moment of need.
- **No integration work**: Upload your existing API spec, go through the wizard, publish. No SDKs, no webhooks, no agent-specific code.
- **Full control over what agents can do**: Allow search and booking but require human approval for appointments. Set your own scopes, rate limits, and policies.
- **Complete safety**: All requests are proxied through AgentCafe. Your backend systems, technician schedules, and customer data stay behind your own firewall.
- **Free to join**: No listing fee, no commission from AgentCafe. Your existing pricing and business model are unchanged.

## The Bottom Line

A home services marketplace that joins AgentCafe becomes the default answer when millions of AI agents need to book a plumber, electrician, or cleaner. Zero cost, zero risk, maximum upside.
